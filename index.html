Create Characters

Choose a wardrobe. Shape their body. Color and shade everything. Define their stats. Select their skills. Edit their AI. Craft your Heroes and then keep going. Create allies, enemies, quest givers and bosses. Whoever you need to populate your Adventures.

Create Skills

What kind of attack will you create? Melee? Projectile? Will it do fire damage or electrical? How about a chance to stun or do damage over time? Maybe you prefer support skills. You can create buffs, heals, shields, and many more.

Create Campaigns

Take your characters and send them on Adventures! Create worlds. Fill them with characters and quests. What stories will you tell?

Share Your Creations

Upload your Characters and Campaigns for others to play. Find inspiration from the community for your next adventure.

Play
